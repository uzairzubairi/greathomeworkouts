The Great Home Workouts website was created by Uzair Zubairi in Mississauga, Ontario. One of the main reasons behind it was Uzair's interest in maintaining good health and remaining physically strong without forking large sums of money to the gym's counter.

Another reason is Uzair's belief that staying strong and healthy should be a lifestyle and not a luxury for rich people only.

Uzair personally edits this website and adds new ideas about how to make the most out of your body's own weight and locked up potential.
